<p><p>The automotive intake manifold market is categorized into three primary types: aluminum, plastic, and magnesium. Aluminum manifolds are valued for their durability and heat resistance, making them suitable for high-performance engines. Plastic manifolds, being lightweight and cost-effective, are increasingly favored for mass production vehicles due to their corrosion resistance. Magnesium manifolds offer a balance of weight and strength but are less common due to cost and production challenges. Each material is tailored to specific vehicle performance and manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
