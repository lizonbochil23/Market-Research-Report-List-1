<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum gratings are versatile materials utilized for flooring, walkways, and safety applications. Press-locked gratings are created by pressing the crossbars into the bearing bars, forming a secure lock without welding, enhancing stability and load capacity. Swage-locked gratings, on the other hand, utilize a swaging process to mechanically lock the crossbars into the bearing bars, offering durability and ease of installation. Both types provide strength, lightweight characteristics, and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
