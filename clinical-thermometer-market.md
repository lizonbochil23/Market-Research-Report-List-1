<p><p>The clinical thermometer market is categorized into three main types: Electronic Clinical Thermometers, Infrared Clinical Thermometers, and Glass Clinical Thermometers. </p><p>Electronic Clinical Thermometers provide quick and accurate readings using digital displays. Infrared Clinical Thermometers measure temperature from a distance, often used for surface or forehead readings, making them convenient and non-invasive. Glass Clinical Thermometers, the traditional type, utilize liquid-filled glass tubes for temperature measurement, although their use has declined due to the risk of breakage and mercury exposure. Each type serves specific preferences and clinical needs.</p></p>
