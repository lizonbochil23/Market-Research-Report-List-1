Indoor LED displays are designed for interior environments, featuring high pixel density for superior image quality and brightness suitable for well-lit spaces. They are commonly used in shopping malls, airports, and conference rooms. Outdoor LED displays, on the other hand, are built to withstand environmental conditions, offering higher brightness and durability for advertising and information purposes in open areas like stadiums and billboards.</p></p>
