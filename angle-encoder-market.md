<p><p>The angle encoder market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Encoders** utilize light to detect position, providing high precision and resolution.</p><p>2. **Magnetic Encoders** use magnetic fields to determine angle, known for robustness and resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>3. **Capacitive Encoders** measure changes in capacitance to track position, offering good accuracy in compact designs.</p><p>4. **Inductive Encoders** operate on electromagnetic principles, offering durability and reliability in harsh conditions. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, catering to varying performance needs.</p></p>
