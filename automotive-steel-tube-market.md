<p><p>The automotive steel tube market features two primary types: welded steel tubes and seamless steel tubes. Welded steel tubes are produced by fusing steel pieces together, offering cost-effectiveness and flexibility in design, making them suitable for various automotive applications. Seamless steel tubes, on the other hand, are formed without seams, providing enhanced strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for critical components like fuel systems and exhausts. Both types play vital roles in the overall automotive manufacturing process.</p></p>
