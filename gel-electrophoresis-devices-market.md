<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gel Electrophoresis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gel electrophoresis devices are categorized into two primary types: horizontal and vertical systems. Horizontal gel electrophoresis is typically used for DNA and RNA analysis, allowing samples to migrate through agarose gels in a flat format. This method is ideal for separations requiring lower resolution but higher throughput. In contrast, vertical gel electrophoresis is suited for protein analysis, using polyacrylamide gels in a more compact orientation, which offers improved resolution for smaller sample volumes and complex protein mixtures.</p></p>
