<p><p>The aircraft de-icing market encompasses two primary types: de-icing trucks and de-icing fluids. De-icing trucks are specialized vehicles equipped with spray systems to apply fluid directly onto aircraft surfaces, effectively removing ice and snow. De-icing fluids, categorized into Type I (hot fluids for melting ice) and Type II/III/IV (thicker, freeze-resistant fluids), are used to prevent ice accumulation during ground operations. Together, these solutions ensure safe and efficient aircraft performance in winter conditions.</p></p>
