<p><p>Breast cancer screening equipment encompasses various technologies, primarily film screen mammography and digital mammography. Film screen mammography utilizes traditional X-ray films to capture images of breast tissue, providing a tangible result, but it has limitations in image clarity and storage. Digital mammography, on the other hand, captures images electronically, enhancing image quality and allowing for easier storage and retrieval. This advancement facilitates better detection rates and improved diagnostic accuracy, making it a preferred choice in modern breast cancer screening.</p></p>
