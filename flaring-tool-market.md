<p><ul><li>Double Flaring Tool</li><li>Single Flaring Tool</li><li>Combination Flaring Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flaring tool market comprises three main types: double flaring tools, single flaring tools, and combination flaring tools. Double flaring tools create a two-layer flare for enhanced sealing, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Single flaring tools produce a single-layer flare, suitable for lower pressure systems and simpler installations. Combination flaring tools combine features of both single and double flaring, offering versatility for various applications. Each type caters to different needs in plumbing, HVAC, and automotive sectors, ensuring effective joint connections.</p></p>
