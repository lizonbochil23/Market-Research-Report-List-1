<p><strong>The Universal Head Mounted Temperature Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Wire</li><li>3-Wire</li><li>4-Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal head-mounted temperature transmitters are categorized into three main types based on wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire systems. </p><p>2-wire transmitters require only two wires for power and signal, making them cost-effective and easy to install. 3-wire systems incorporate an additional wire for improved accuracy, compensating for wire resistance. 4-wire transmitters provide the highest accuracy and stability by using separate pairs for power and signal, ideal for long-distance applications in industrial settings. Each type serves specific application needs.</p></p>
