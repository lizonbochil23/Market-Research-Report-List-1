<p><p>The hand dryer market primarily comprises two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-speed jets of air to quickly remove moisture from hands, offering energy efficiency and reduced drying times. In contrast, hot air dryers emit warm air to evaporate water gradually, typically taking longer to dry hands. Both types are popular in public restrooms, with jet air dryers favored for their speed and hygiene, while hot air dryers appeal for their simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
