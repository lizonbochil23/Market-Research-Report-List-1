<p><strong>The Screw Doser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Screw Doser</li><li>Gravimetric Screw Doser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw doser market is primarily divided into two types: volumetric and gravimetric screw dosers. Volumetric screw dosers measure and dispense materials based on a set volume, ensuring consistent delivery but potentially allowing for variations in density. In contrast, gravimetric screw dosers accurately weigh the materials before dispensing, providing precise control and minimizing waste. Both types are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and plastics, where accurate material handling is crucial for efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
