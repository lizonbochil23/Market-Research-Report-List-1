<p><strong>The Tubular Sight Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Thread Type</li><li>Welding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubular sight glass market comprises three main types: flange type, thread type, and welding type. Flange type sight glasses are bolted between flanged pipe sections, ensuring a leak-proof seal. Thread type features threaded ends for easy connection to existing pipelines, offering versatile installation options. Welding type sight glasses are directly welded into the piping system, providing a strong, permanent solution ideal for high-pressure applications. Each type serves specific industry needs based on installation preferences and operational requirements.</p></p>
