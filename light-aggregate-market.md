<p><strong>The Light Aggregate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Lightweight Aggregate</li><li>Artificial Lightweight Aggregate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lightweight aggregate market is divided into two main types: natural and artificial lightweight aggregates. Natural lightweight aggregates are derived from naturally occurring materials, such as pumice, expanded clay, or shale, known for their low density and high insulating properties. In contrast, artificial lightweight aggregates are manufactured from industrial byproducts, such as expanded polystyrene or glass, designed to achieve specific performance characteristics. Both types are utilized in construction to enhance structural efficiency, reduce weight, and improve insulation.</p></p>
