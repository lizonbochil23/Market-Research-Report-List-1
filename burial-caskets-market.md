<p><p>The burial casket market comprises three primary types: veneer wood caskets, solid wood caskets, and metal caskets. Veneer wood caskets are made from a thin layer of wood over a composite material, offering a cost-effective and aesthetically pleasing option. Solid wood caskets are crafted from a single type of wood, providing durability and a traditional appearance. Metal caskets, often made from steel or copper, are valued for their strength and longevity. Each type caters to diverse consumer preferences and budgets.</p></p>
