<p><p>The nitrile rubber market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (HNBR) and Carboxylated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (XNBR). HNBR is known for its superior heat, oil, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications in automotive and industrial sectors. Both types cater to distinct industry needs, driving their market growth.</p></p>
