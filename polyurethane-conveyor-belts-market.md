<p><p>The polyurethane conveyor belts market can be categorized mainly into two types: TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) and PUR (Polyurethane). TPU conveyor belts are known for their high resistance to abrasion, cutting, and chemicals, making them ideal for demanding applications in industries like automotive and food processing. PUR conveyor belts, while similar, offer enhanced flexibility and durability in various temperatures, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial environments. Both types contribute to efficiency and longevity in material handling processes.</p></p>
