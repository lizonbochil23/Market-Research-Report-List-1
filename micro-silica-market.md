<p><p>Micro Silica, also known as Silica Fume, is a byproduct of silicon metal or ferrosilicon alloy production. It consists of very fine particles of siliceous material, which significantly enhances the properties of concrete and cement. <p><ul><li>Densified Micro Silica</li><li>Semi Densified Micro Silica</li><li>Undensified Micro Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro silica market is categorized into three main types: densified micro silica, semi-densified micro silica, and undensified micro silica. Densified micro silica involves a compacted form that enhances performance in concrete and construction applications. Semi-densified micro silica offers a balance between flowability and density, making it suitable for varied industrial uses. Undensified micro silica, with its low density, is often used for applications requiring high surface area and reactivity, such as in cement and composite materials.</p></p>
