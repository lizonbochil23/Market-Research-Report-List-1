<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OLED Television Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED)</li><li>PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED television market is primarily divided into two types: AMOLED and PMOLED. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) utilizes a grid of individual pixels controlled by transistors, allowing for faster refresh rates and enhanced brightness, making it ideal for high-resolution displays. In contrast, PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) employs a simpler grid layout, where rows and columns are activated sequentially, resulting in slower response times and lower resolution. While PMOLED is suitable for smaller, less demanding applications, AMOLED dominates the premium TV segment.</p></p>
