<p><p>The veterinary dental scalers market comprises two primary types: ultrasonic and mechanical scalers. Ultrasonic scalers utilize high-frequency vibrations to remove plaque and tartar efficiently, making them ideal for quick and effective dental cleanings. In contrast, mechanical scalers rely on manual operation, using hand-held instruments for scaling and cleaning teeth, providing greater control but requiring more time and skill. Both types are essential for maintaining the oral health of animals in veterinary practices.</p></p>
