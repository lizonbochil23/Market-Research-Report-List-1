<p><p>The flight tracking system market comprises various types, including ADS-B, FANS, and PFTS. ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast) uses satellite navigation to enable aircraft to broadcast their position, enhancing situational awareness. FANS (Future Air Navigation System) facilitates air traffic control communication and enhances safety in oceanic and remote regions. PFTS (Precision Flight Tracking System) provides real-time tracking of aircraft, ensuring timely and accurate data for both airlines and regulatory agencies, ultimately improving operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
