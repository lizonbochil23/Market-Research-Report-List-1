<p><p>The disposable medical nitrile gloves market is categorized into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered nitrile gloves are coated with a powder, typically cornstarch, to facilitate easy donning and removal, making them user-friendly. However, they may pose allergy risks and are being phased out in certain applications. Non-powdered nitrile gloves, on the other hand, do not contain any powder, reducing the risk of allergic reactions and making them suitable for sensitive environments, including surgical and laboratory settings.</p></p>
