<p><p>The siphonic drainage system market divides into two main types: internal and external drainage systems. Internal drainage systems operate within a building, utilizing siphonic principles to efficiently remove wastewater through a network of pipes that rely on negative pressure. Conversely, external drainage systems are installed outside structures, managing surface runoff and stormwater using siphonic technology to enhance flow capacity. Both systems focus on optimizing drainage efficiency while minimizing environmental impact and improving urban water management strategies.</p></p>
