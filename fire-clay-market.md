<p><p>The fire clay market comprises various types, including flint, semi-flint, semi-plastic, and plastic fire clay. Flint fire clay is characterized by low plasticity and high refractory properties, making it ideal for use in high-temperature applications. Semi-flint offers a balance of plasticity and strength, suitable for a range of industrial uses. Semi-plastic fire clay possesses higher plasticity, providing better molding capabilities. Plastic fire clay features maximum plasticity, making it suitable for shaping and forming intricate designs in ceramics and pottery.</p></p>
