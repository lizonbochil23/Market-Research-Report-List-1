<p><p>The cellular imaging system market encompasses various advanced microscopy techniques. Single Molecule Localization Microscopy (SMLM) allows visualization of individual molecules, enhancing resolution beyond traditional limits. Structured Illumination Microscopy (SIM) improves imaging by using patterned light, enhancing resolution while minimizing photodamage. Other methodologies include fluorescence and electron microscopy, which serve diverse applications in cellular biology. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to specific research needs in areas such as drug development, disease study, and cellular behavior analysis.</p></p>
