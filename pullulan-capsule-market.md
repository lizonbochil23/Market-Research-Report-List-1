<p><p>Pullulan capsules are a type of vegetarian capsule made from the polysaccharide pullulan, which is derived from the fermentation of cornstarch. These capsules are gaining popularity in the pharmaceutical and dietary supplement industries due to their plant-based nature, offering an alternative to gelatin capsules. They are known for their excellent oxygen-barrier properties, stability, and ability to dissolve quickly in the stomach.</p><p>The Pullulan Capsule Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing consumer preference for vegetarian and vegan products, coupled with the rising awareness regarding the benefits of natural ingredients in supplements.
