<p><p>The examination couches market can be categorized into three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric examination couches offer adjustable positions at the touch of a button, providing convenience and efficiency for healthcare providers. Manual couches require physical adjustment, often making them more cost-effective but less user-friendly. Hydraulic examination couches utilize a hydraulic lift system, allowing height adjustments with minimal effort, ideal for various medical settings. Each type serves distinct needs based on usability, functionality, and budget considerations.</p></p>
