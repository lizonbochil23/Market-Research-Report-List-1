<p><ul><li>Active In-Ceiling Speaker</li><li>Passive In-Ceiling Speaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-ceiling speaker market is divided into two main types: active and passive in-ceiling speakers. Active in-ceiling speakers come with built-in amplification, allowing them to connect directly to audio sources without the need for an external amplifier. This design simplifies installation and setup. Conversely, passive in-ceiling speakers require an external amplifier to power them, typically offering greater flexibility for complex audio systems. Both types cater to different user needs, preferences, and usage scenarios in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
