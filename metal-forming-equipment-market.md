<p><strong>The Metal Forming Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal forming equipment market is categorized into three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping large metal pieces through processes like forging and extrusion, focusing on strength and structural integrity. Sheet forming pertains to manipulating thin metal sheets via techniques such as stamping and bending, ideal for producing intricate shapes. Powder metal forming involves compacting metal powders into desired shapes and sintering them, offering high precision and material efficiency for specialized applications.</p></p>
