<p><ul><li>Voltage Mode Control</li><li>Current Mode Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-frequency switching-mode power supply market is primarily categorized into two types: voltage mode control and current mode control. Voltage mode control maintains output voltage by adjusting the duty cycle of the switching elements, providing stability and simplicity. In contrast, current mode control regulates the output by monitoring the inductor current, enhancing response to load changes and improving protection against overcurrent. Both types cater to diverse applications, offering efficiency and compact designs in modern electronic systems.</p></p>
