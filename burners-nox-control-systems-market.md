<p><p>The Burners NOx Control Systems market primarily encompasses two technologies: Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). SNCR involves injecting urea or ammonia into flue gas at high temperatures to convert nitrogen oxides (NOx) into nitrogen and water vapor, typically used in industries with large combustion sources. SCR employs a catalyst to enhance the reaction efficiency, allowing for lower operating temperatures and more effective NOx reduction, making it suitable for various industrial applications and power generation.</p></p>
