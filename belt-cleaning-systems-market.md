<p><ul><li>Scraper</li><li>Brush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt cleaning systems are essential for maintaining efficiency in conveyor operations across various industries. The two primary types are scraper and brush cleaners. Scraper systems utilize sharp blades to effectively remove bulk materials and debris from the belt surface, ensuring minimal carryback. In contrast, brush cleaners employ rotating bristle brushes to gently dislodge fine particles and prevent buildup, catering to more delicate applications. Both systems enhance operational performance, prolong equipment life, and contribute to overall workplace safety and cleanliness.</p></p>
