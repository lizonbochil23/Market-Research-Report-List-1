<p><p>The Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor market consists of various types that monitor fluid temperature for optimal transmission performance. Bimetallic temperature sensors use two metals to detect temperature changes. Temperature Sensor ICs offer integrated circuits for precise measurements. Thermistors are resistive devices sensitive to temperature changes. RTDs provide accurate readings through resistance variations. Thermocouples generate voltage in response to temperature differences. Infrared sensors measure temperature without contact, while fiber optic sensors utilize light transmission for non-invasive monitoring. Other types may include advanced or hybrid technologies.</p></p>
