<p><p>The SSL certification market comprises three main types: Domain Validated (DV) SSL, Organization Validated (OV) SSL, and Extended Validation (EV) SSL certificates. DV SSL certificates verify domain ownership, offering basic security suitable for personal websites. OV SSL certificates provide stronger authentication by validating the organization behind the domain, ideal for business sites. EV SSL certificates offer the highest level of assurance, displaying the organization's name prominently, making them ideal for e-commerce and banking sites, enhancing trust among users.</p></p>
