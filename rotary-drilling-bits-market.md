<p><strong>The Rotary Drilling Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller-Cone Bits</li><li>Fixed-Cutter Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary drilling bits market is primarily divided into two types: roller-cone bits and fixed-cutter bits. Roller-cone bits feature rotating cones that crush and grind the rock, making them suitable for various formations. They excel in soft to medium-hard rock conditions. Fixed-cutter bits, on the other hand, utilize a stationary design with cutting elements like polycrystalline diamond compact (PDC), ideal for harder formations. Both types are crucial for efficient drilling operations in oil, gas, and mineral exploration sectors.</p></p>
