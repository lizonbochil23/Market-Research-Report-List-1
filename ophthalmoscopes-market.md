<p><strong>The Ophthalmoscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Inspection</li><li>Indirect Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmoscope market is primarily categorized into two types: direct and indirect inspection. Direct inspection ophthalmoscopes allow practitioners to visualize the retina and optic nerve head directly, offering a clear view of the fundus with a focused beam of light. In contrast, indirect inspection ophthalmoscopes provide a broader view of the retina by using a light source and a lens to create an upright, virtual image, enabling the examination of peripheral retinal areas. Both types play crucial roles in ophthalmic assessments.</p></p>
