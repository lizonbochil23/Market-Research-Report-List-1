<p><p>The game console market can be categorized into two main types: handle control and somatosensory control. Handle control systems utilize traditional gamepads or controllers, enabling precise input with buttons and sticks for various genres. In contrast, somatosensory control systems focus on motion detection and physical user interactions, allowing players to engage through body movements and gestures. Both market types cater to different gaming experiences, with handle control appealing to competitive gamers and somatosensory control targeting more casual or family-oriented gaming.</p></p>
